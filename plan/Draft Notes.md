#### **1\. Predictive Inventory Management (Cost Reduction)**

By analyzing the correlation between game types (e.g., rivalries vs. family nights) and specific food sales, the SOFMC can minimize risk by preventing overbuying or underbuying of perishable goods.

* **Impact:** Reduces waste and ensures popular items don't sell out during peak times.

#### **2\. Maximizing Sales Velocity (Revenue Growth)**

The data highlights bottlenecks at concessions. Identifying which POS locations have the highest wait times allows for tactical shifts, such as deploying mobile vendors or adjusting staffing levels to increase the "velocity of sales" during short intermission windows.

* **Impact:** Captures revenue from fans who would otherwise abandon a long line to return to their seats.

#### **3\. Strategic "Basket of Goods" Increases (Revenue Growth)**

By understanding the correlation between certain food and alcohol purchases, the team can implement pricing strategies or "bundles" (e.g., a "Power Play Combo") to increase the average transaction value.

* **Impact:** Increases the total spend per customer per visit.

#### **4\. Targeted Customer Acquisition & Retention (Revenue Growth)**

Identifying the "main avatar" or power fans from the ticket data allows for personalized marketing. If the data shows a high-value fan hasn't attended in a month, an automated retention offer can be triggered.

* **Impact:** Improves customer success and long-term loyalty.
